Identification of organic compounds containing one functional group out of the following:-
<p style="padding-left: 30px;">Alcohols- Methyl Alcohol, Ethyl Alcohol, Isopopyl Alcohol &amp; Tert-butyl Alcohol.
Carboxylic Acids: Formic acid, Acetic Acid, Oxalic Acid, Benzoic Acid, Salicylic Acid, Picric Acid etc.
Carbonyl Compounds: Formaldehyde, Acetaldehyde, Acetone, Benzaldehyde, Acetophenone etc.</p>
<p style="padding-left: 30px;">Miscellaneous: Phenols, Aniline, Nitro-benzene, Chloro-benzene, Urea Benzamide, Acetanalide, Acetamide etc.</p>
<strong>Various types of titration:</strong>
<p style="padding-left: 30px;">Standardization of KMn04 Solution with Standard Oxalic acid or Sodium oxalate.
Estimation of Fe+2 Iron with Standard KMn04 Solution.
Standardization of Sodium thio Sulphate Solution with standard K2Cr202 Solution.
Determination of available Chlorine in bleaching powder by Iodometric method.
Determination of Na2C03 content of Washing Soda.
Determination of Strength of H O
Determination of total Acid and Alkali in Soap.
Determination of hardness of water.
Removal of hardness by different methods.</p>
